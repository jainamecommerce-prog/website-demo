## Tone of Voice

- **Professional but approachable** — not too corporate, not too casual
- **Action-oriented** — use verbs: automate, build, streamline, scale
- **Beginner-friendly** — avoid heavy technical jargon in client-facing copy

---

## Services

1. **Workflow Automation** — Design and deploy automated business workflows
2. **n8n Automation** — Custom n8n pipelines for data, emails, CRMs, and more
3. **Claude AI Integration** — Connect Claude AI into existing tools and processes
